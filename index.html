About Us

Who We Are

Roy'Han supplies institutional-grade soaps, detergents and air fresheners to hospitals, schools, hotels, restaurants and factories across the region. Beyond the products we sell, we run a weekly community programme that cleans local masjids using donations from our customers and partners — because the hygiene we sell for a living is a service we believe in giving back.

Our Mission

Deliver dependable, high-performance hygiene supplies to every institution we serve, without compromise.

Our Values

Reliability, cleanliness and community — we treat every client site and every masjid we wash the same way: with care.

Our Promise

A portion of what we earn goes straight back into keeping places of worship in our community clean, every week.
